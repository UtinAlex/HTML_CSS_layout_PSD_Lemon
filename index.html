Bananas Foster Ice Cream Cace

🕒 35mins 📶 Easy SAVE RECIPE

1(8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
20 chocolate round wafers, divided
1(7 1/4 ounce) bottle Smucker's ® Hot Dark Chocolate Microwaveable Top-
ping, divided

Directions

ARRANGE 8 to 9 ice cream sandwiches in 9-inch square pan, cutting to
from an even layer. Spread with half of whipped topping. Break or crush
chocolate wafers and sprinkle over the whiped cream. Microwave 1 cup of
chocolate topping into small microwave-safe bowl on HIGH for 30 seconds.
Drizzle evenly over wafers.
TOP with remaining 9 to 9 ice cream sandwiches to from an even layer.
Spread with remaining whipped toping. Freeze 3 hours or overnight. Remove
